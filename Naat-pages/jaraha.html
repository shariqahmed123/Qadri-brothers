JASHNE AMADE RASOOL ALLAH HI

Jasne amade Rasoole, Allah hi Allah
Bibi Amna ke phool, Allah hi Allah Jab kay Sarkaar tashrief lane lage,
Hoero milma bhi gushian mana ne laqe,
Har taraf noor ki rosheni chagayi,
Mustafa kiya milay zindagi milgayi,
Aie Halima teri goad mein aa gaye,
Dono alam ke Rasool, Allah hee Allah Koi chehray ki tareef kernay laga,
Koi zulfoun key tarif kernay laga,
Aminah dekh ker muskuranay lagein,
Hawwaa, Mariam bhi khushiyaan mananay lagein,
Amina bibi sab say yeh kehnay lagein,
Dua ho gayi qabool, Allah he Allah. Shadiyaanay kushi kay bajaye gaye,
Shaad kay naghmay sab ko sunaye gaye,
Har taraf shor Salealla ho gaya,
Aaj peda Habib e Khuda ho gaya,
Phir to Jibriel nay bhi yeh elaan kiya,
Yeh Khuda kay hein Rasool, Allah hi Allah. Jashn-e-Aamade Rasool, Allah he Allah,
Bibi Amna kay phool, Allah he Allah.
